ABOUT US

Industrial Training Institute, Golaghat is a Govt. Institute commonly known as ITI Golaghat. It is the one of the top-tier institute of Golaghat, and under the administrative control of the Directorate of Employment and Craftsmen Training, Assam and Ministry of Skill, Employment and Entrepreneurship, Government of Assam. The Institute was started in the year 2018 at Pulibor, Golaghat. The institute was modernized under World Bank Project. As per order released from DGE&T by letter no.-(DGE&T-19(4)/2011-CD-Pt Dated-17th January 2013)all the existing trades in Golaghat ITI have been implemented in the semester.
